Walking Your Path

Congrats! You’ve decided to embrace the world of education. I am so proud of you. Think about how incredible this moment is. You’re about to undergo a life-changing event, so make sure you remember this moment well. I’m going to be honest, it’s going to be intimidating. I came from a reservation to a city, so not only did I have to deal with understanding how to manage college life, but I also had to adjust to living in a city with people from diverse backgrounds. It can be lonely, so make sure you have a steady person with whom you can connect. If you want in-person support, look to see if your school offers an indigenous connection program. But I know you’ve got this, I mean, look at how far you’ve come right now.
