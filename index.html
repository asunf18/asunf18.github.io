Welcome

Ya’ahteeh, this website is a space for curious indigenous students to explore resources and find information. We hope that you find what you’re looking for, whether you’re considering attending college or are in the process of doing so. Explore our website for personalized insights into higher education, designed to help you navigate this exciting transition with confidence!
